Tinysat Viz: Sudoku

This demonstration visualizes how a basic CDCL SAT solving algorithm finds solutions for Sudoku puzzles, with a basic encoding of the rules of Sudoku into SAT.

Select an example Sudoku puzzle (or enter your own).
Select the demonstration speed, and click "Run!" to start.

When the demonstration runs, the current state of the search is depicted on the Sudoku board, and the learned clauses are shown below the board.
